EVASH | 益华世科技

Always Remember P

EVASH, also known as 益华世科技 in China, originates from the USA with over 30 years of history, and is a leading brand for high-performance storage solutions.

Brand origin and meaning:

  • EVASH: stands for "Embedded Value & Advanced Storage Hardware", symbolizing innovative memory technology and high reliability.
  • When entering China, the brand was named 益华世:
    • Yi (益): creating value and benefits for customers and partners
    • Hua (华): representing excellence and Chinese heritage
    • Shi (世): symbolizing global vision and long-term reliability
  • Combined with UWL EVASH / 益华世, it highlights global brand identity with local trust.

EVASH is easy to remember, representing innovation, reliability, and high-performance memory solutions.

EVASH 益华世科技，源自美国，已有超过30年的历史，是高性能存储解决方案的领先品牌。

品牌名称来源与寓意：

  • EVASH：来源于“Embedded Value & Advanced Storage Hardware”，象征创新存储技术与高可靠性能。
  • 进入中国市场后，取名为 益华世：
    • 益：为客户和合作伙伴创造价值与利益
    • 华：代表卓越品质与中国背景
    • 世：象征全球视野和长期可靠性
  • 结合 UWL 益华世，体现全球化品牌标识与本土化信任感。

EVASH 简洁易记，代表创新、可靠与高性能存储芯片解决方案。
